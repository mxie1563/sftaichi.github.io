Meet Your Instructor & Community

Sifu Michael Xie

Master of the Internal Arts

Sifu Michael has been a devoted practitioner of Tai Chi Chuan for over 28 years. He specializes in the gentle, yet powerful, **Hun Yuan Chen Style**. His teaching philosophy focuses on making the complex art accessible to all ages, emphasizing proper structure and meditative focus over strenuous effort.
